Featured Grammar Point

N5 Level

〜ています

Present continuous form

The 〜ています form is used to express actions that are currently in progress (present continuous) or states that continue from the past to the present.

Formation:
  1. Take the verb in its ます form
  2. Change ます to て
  3. Add います

Example transformation:

食べます → 食べて → 食べています

tabemasu → tabete → tabete imasu

Usage:
  • To express an action in progress:
    今、本を読んでいます。
    Ima, hon o yonde imasu.
    I am reading a book now.
  • To express a continuing state:
    私は東京に住んでいます。
    Watashi wa Tokyo ni sunde imasu.
    I live in Tokyo. (continuing state)
