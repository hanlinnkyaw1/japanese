JLPT Burmese follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. Google is a third-party vendor on our site. It uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to jlptburmese.com and other sites on the internet. You may choose to decline the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Our Advertising Partners

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements. These third-party ad servers use technologies like cookies or JavaScript to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and to personalize the advertising content you see.

If you contact us directly for Japanese language course updates, we may receive information such as your name and email address. We use this strictly to provide the requested support or learning materials.

5. CCPA and GDPR Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the right to request disclosure, correction, or deletion of any personal data we have collected. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Contact Us

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through the official contact links on our homepage.
